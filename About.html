About The System

This system is designed to manage the inventory of a food truck. It allows the user to keep track of the products on the menu, orders received, and the availability of those products. The system also includes a calendar to keep track of the events and promotions that the food truck host.

System functionalities

  • Manage the inventory of the food truck
  • Keep track of orders received
  • Manage the calendar of events and promotions
  • Allow the user to update the menu and pricing
  • Generate reports on the sales and inventory levels
